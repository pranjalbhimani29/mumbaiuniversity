Campus Life at Mumbai University

Campus Facilities

Library

A spacious library with over 100,000 books and journals for students to enhance their learning experience.

Labs

State-of-the-art laboratories equipped with the latest technology for engineering research and innovation.

Hostels

Comfortable and secure accommodation for students, with Wi-Fi and recreational facilities.

Cafeteria

A vibrant cafeteria offering healthy food options for students, with diverse cuisines and snacks.

Student Activities

  • Cultural Fests: Annual cultural events bringing together students for performances, music, and dance.
  • Technical Fests: Inter-departmental technical events showcasing innovations and projects.
  • Hackathons: A platform for coding enthusiasts to solve real-world challenges in 24 hours.
  • Workshops: Regular workshops on emerging technologies, soft skills, and professional development.
